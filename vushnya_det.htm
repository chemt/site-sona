Kuhn's Jewelers

E-commerce website for the nation's 6th oldest jewelry store

Project Summary

Kuhn's Jewelers has over a century and a half of experience in the jewelry business. That's why they knew it was important to reach out to Sunrise Design and have us create a web presence that didn't look like "every other jewelry website". Kuhn's is located in a historic part of downtown Salisbury and is the sixth oldest continuously operated jeweler in America.

When Kuhn's contacted us, they told us that they wanted the ability to sell their products online and compete with the big guys. Kuhn's has an extremely loyal customer base and wanted the website to offer the same type of customer service as the store does. The website offers free shipping and free gift wrapping on all orders.
